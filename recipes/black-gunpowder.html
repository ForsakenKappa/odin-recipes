Gunpowder!

Goes boom!

So, my little anarchist, you want to make some things go BOOM? Then I'm glad you came across this website, because you'll learn how to make a gunpowder! Here are some of the ingridients you would need:
  • Stick x1
  • 15 carrat diamond x2
  • Your grandfather's workbench
  • Sapper suit
I hope you got all the stuff to get some gundpowder! But a quick warning:

Gunpowder is highly explosive! Don't just get it because you can! And procces of getting it is extremely dangerous!

So, here are the steps to get some explosive sand:
  1. Wait until midnight
  2. Using your stick and two diamonds craft a sword
  3. Put on sapper suit
  4. Whilst equiped with a sword go outside into the darkness. You are looking for a green penis-shaped hellish abomination.
  5. If you found one, try to sneak up to it and quickly slay with your sword. Beware, that green thing would and will explode when provoked
  6. If you didn't found one of them, keep looking. They appear only in full darkness.
  7. After you killed one of them, they will turn into pure black gundpowder
  8. Enjoy making TNT with it and some sand! Beware of admins and mods, they could ban you if you overdo the BOOM part.
